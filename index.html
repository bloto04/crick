Aunt Fatima

Aunt Fatima is a horror game.

A game where you play the role of a kidnapped person by Aunt Fatima and her husband, who found a way to escape from the house. Aunt Fatima and her husband kidnap people, force them to stream on the dark web, then kill them and sell their organs on the black market. You are the only one who managed to find a way to escape from the house.
